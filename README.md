# Career-Track-Analysis-with-SQL-and-Tableau
[Github Page with Dashboard](https://didiu132.github.io/Career-Track-Analysis-with-SQL-and-Tableau/)

This project analyzes the career track enrollments and achievements of 365’s students
The tracks represent an ordinal sequence of courses that eventually lead to obtaining the skills for one of three job titles: data scientist, data analyst, or business analyst.

## Key Insights and Recommendations
- Enrollments and completions spike in August, likely due to students preparing for the school year
- The Data Analysis track has by far the most enrollments but has the lowest proportion of completion. More investigation should be done on why students are not completing the track
- Most tracks are completed within 91 to 365 days, so yearly subscriptions would be the most appropriate for students
- Our overall completion ratio is 1.18% of enrollments, which is quite low. More research into the reasons for low completion needs to be carried out.
